## Curation Routine (45 minutes, every Monday)

1. **Sunday evening (15 min):** Scan email newsletters — Import AI, The Batch, Ben's Bites.
2. **Monday morning (20 min):** Check MIT Tech Review, The Gradient for the past week.
3. **Monday writing (10 min):** Fill in 5 summaries, assign buckets and day hints.

## Tagging Conventions

- Keep tags lowercase, no spaces: `llm`, `enterprise`, `regulation`, `healthcare`
- Max 3 tags per item
- Common tags: `openai`, `google`, `enterprise`, `consulting`, `healthcare`, `legal`, `finance`, `copyright`
